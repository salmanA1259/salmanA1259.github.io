Intro

Greetings, I am an individual driven by dedication and passion, equipped with a diverse skill set that extends across various creative and analytical domains. My journey in Data Science has been shaped by an unwavering curiosity and an ongoing commitment to learning.

Having built a strong foundation in Data Analytics, Machine Learning, GIS, and remote sensing, I excel in dynamic environments that demand adaptability and innovation. My expertise encompasses Power BI, Data Wrangling, Data Preprocessing, Data Analysis, and Data Visualization using Python programming. Throughout my professional experiences, I have consistently demonstrated a robust work ethic, effective communication, and a collaborative mindset.
